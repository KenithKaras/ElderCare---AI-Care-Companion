# 👵 Elder Care Assistant (ECA)

Elder Care Assistant is a compassionate, AI-powered platform designed to help senior citizens live independently while keeping their families and caregivers connected in real-time.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🏡 For Seniors (Independent Living)
- **Emergency SOS**: A one-tap "Panic Button" that notifies your entire care network immediately with your GPS location.
- **Smart Pharmacy**: A simple list of your medications with reminders. Just tap "Take" when you're done.
- **Mind & Joy (AI-Wellness)**:
  - **AI Chat**: Ask health questions or just have a friendly conversation.
  - **Brain Games**: Memory matching games to keep your mind sharp.
  - **Guided Calm**: Soothing meditation sessions generated in real-time.
- **Health Logs**: Easy tracking of Blood Pressure, Sugar, and Heart Rate.

### 👩‍⚕️ For Caregivers (Peace of Mind)
- **Live Monitoring**: See the senior's vitals, steps, and medication status in real-time.
- **Family Chat**: A private, secure message channel with AI-suggested quick replies.
- **Safety Map**: Track the last shared location of your loved one.
- **Remote Setup**: Add medications or appointments to the senior's calendar from your own device.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Sign Up**: Choose your role (Elder or Caregiver).
2. **Care ID**: Every user gets a 5-digit ID (e.g., #10522).
3. **Link**: The Caregiver enters the Senior's ID in the "Profile" page to connect the two accounts.
4. **Connect Devices**: Go to the **Devices** page to link your Smart Watch (Bluetooth) or Google Fit account.

## ⌚ Hardware Connectivity
ECA supports real-time heart rate monitoring. 
1. Open the **Devices** page.
2. Ensure your watch is in "Broadcast" or "Pairing" mode.
3. Tap **Smart Watch / Bluetooth HRM** and select your device.
